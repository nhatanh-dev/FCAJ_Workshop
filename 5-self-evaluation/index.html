Self-Assessment

During my time participating in the First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ) - AWS training program from January 5th to April 3rd, I had the valuable opportunity to study AWS services in-depth at the office and directly apply this knowledge to a practical project. This experience helped me take my first steps into a professional tech environment and corporate culture.

I collaborated with my team members to develop the SmartInvoice project. Through this hands-on experience, I significantly improved practical skills such as Cloud architecture design, self-learning and integrating new technologies, and Agile teamwork.

Regarding my work ethic, I highly value self-directed learning and strictly adhere to the rules and schedules set by the organizers and mentors. During the project development phase, I primarily worked closely with my team members, engaging in continuous communication to collaboratively solve problems and achieve our shared goals.

Key Strengths & Achievements

  • Teamwork & Collaboration: I possess a strong sense of responsibility toward shared goals, always ready to support teammates in overcoming challenges, and capable of fostering an open, comfortable communication environment within the team.
  • Learning & Practical Application: By taking careful notes, I have firmly grasped the core concepts of the AWS ecosystem. Applying this theoretical knowledge directly to the SmartInvoice project has deepened my practical understanding of how these services operate in real-world scenarios.
  • Communication & Networking: I am sociable and proactive in building connections. I successfully made many new friends within the program and actively networked with experienced industry professionals during tech events.
  • Strict Discipline: I fully comply with all office regulations, maintaining a professional workspace without causing any disturbances or noise that might affect those around me.

Areas for Improvement

  • Proactiveness in Seeking Help: I am still quite hesitant to ask for help when facing roadblocks. Instead of reaching out to mentors for guidance, I often spend too much time searching the internet for solutions. I need to learn how to better leverage the support of experienced seniors.
  • Verbal Communication & Articulation: I sometimes struggle to accurately express my thoughts in words. I need to practice organizing my sentences and structuring logical arguments to convey technical ideas clearly and comprehensibly.
  • Time Management: I have not yet found the optimal balance between studying AWS theory and executing project tasks, which sometimes leads to schedule conflicts. I need to develop a more structured and scientific approach to time allocation.
  • Problem-Solving Mindset: I need to cultivate broader systemic thinking and practice more flexible problem-solving approaches to better align with the rigorous standards of an enterprise environment.

Future Career Goals

  • Deepen Cloud Computing Expertise: Continue to study and research the AWS ecosystem extensively, moving beyond basic usage to fully understand the underlying architectural principles.
  • Aim for a higher Role: My long-term career goal is to go beyond being a standard Software Engineer. I aspire to equip myself with a comprehensive system vision to become a Solution Architect—capable of designing optimal architectures while continuously expanding a strong professional network within the tech industry.

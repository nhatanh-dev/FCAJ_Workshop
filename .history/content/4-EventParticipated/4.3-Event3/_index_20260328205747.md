# FCAJ Community Day 2026

### Event Overview

The **FCAJ Community Day** was the first major AWS Community event of the year, bringing together current members and FCAJ alumni who are now professionals at leading tech companies like GotyMeX, Sympli, Katalon, VPBank, and Renova Cloud. The core theme of the event centered around the latest industry trends, specifically the intersection of DevOps, AI operations, and the future of autonomous agents. 

### Key Sessions & Technical Insights

Unlike standard public events, this gathering was deeply technical and tailored to practitioners, featuring insights directly from the production environments of enterprise companies.

#### 1. GotyMeX: Company Culture & Tech Talent Development
The event opened with a session by Mr. Hai Bui and Mr. Phuc Dang, Software Development Managers at GotyMeX. They provided a comprehensive overview of the company's tech stack, ongoing projects, and their Intern/Fresher talent development program. The presentation highlighted a highly collaborative, friendly, and open engineering culture. The structured Intern/Fresher roadmap was particularly relevant, offering a clear professional orientation that strongly aligns with my current career planning and goals for post-graduation integration into the enterprise environment.

#### 2. Platform Engineering, DevOps & GenAIOps
A standout technical deep-dive was delivered collaboratively by Nghi Danh Hoang Hieu (AI Engineer) and Phong Nguyen (Software Engineer). They merged their respective expertise to cover the entire pipeline of modern AI applications:
* **AI Development Practices:** Nghi shared best practices for building AI tools like chatbots and voicebots, emphasizing the importance of pipeline thinking in AI development.
* **DevOps Integration:** Phong connected these AI concepts with deep DevOps expertise, introducing the concept of **GenAIOps**—the practice of automating and scaling Generative AI models in production.

Seeing Nghi—a fellow 2005-born peer who started his FCAJ journey two years ago—confidently deliver complex theoretical and practical knowledge was both a source of peer pressure and immense inspiration. While the advanced GenAIOps pipelines are currently beyond my foundational knowledge of DevOps, understanding the architectural concepts provides a critical blueprint for how systems like our SmartInvoice project could be scaled and deployed in the future.

#### 3. The Rise of the Agentic Era
Mr. Phạm Tiến Thuận Phát (Software Engineer) delivered a highly engaging presentation on the "Rise of the Agentic Era." He emphasized how the industry is rapidly shifting from passive AI tools to active **AI Agents** capable of autonomous decision-making and task automation. Given the recurring theme of "Agents" in recent tech discussions, this session effectively mapped out the trajectory of where software automation is heading, solidifying the importance of mastering these integrations early on.

### Community & Networking

The presence of various AWS Cloud Clubs (HUFLIT, UTE, Saigon University, PTIT) and seasoned industry professionals created a large-scale, dense environment. While the sheer volume of advanced technical information was somewhat overwhelming, it sparked highly productive discussions between my friends and me. Instead of broad networking, we focused on dissecting the speakers' topics at our table, trying to connect the GenAIOps and Agentic concepts back to the systems we are currently building.

> **Conclusion:** The FCAJ Community Day successfully demonstrated the massive gap between building a simple AI feature and maintaining a robust GenAIOps pipeline in production. It set a high technical bar and provided a clear, inspiring roadmap for what is achievable with consistent learning.

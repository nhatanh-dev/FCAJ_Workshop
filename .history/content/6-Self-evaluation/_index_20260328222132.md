I collaborated with my team members to develop the **SmartInvoice** project. Through this hands-on experience, I significantly improved practical skills such as Cloud architecture design, self-learning and integrating new technologies, and Agile teamwork.

Regarding my work ethic, I highly value self-directed learning and strictly adhere to the rules and schedules set by the organizers and mentors.
